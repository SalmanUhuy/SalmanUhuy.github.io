Society Renewable Energy ITS (SRE ITS)

Society of Renewable Energy Institut Teknologi Sepuluh Nopember (SRE ITS) exists as student organization dedicated to increasing student interest and knowledge of new and renewable energy. SRE ITS exists to encourage students at ITS and throught Indonesia to be concerned about the renewable energy sector
